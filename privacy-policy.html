Information We Collect
Section 1

When you use CollegePIPE's college predictor tool, we collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Full Name — to personalise your report
  • Email Address — to identify your account and deliver your report
  • Mobile Number — for order confirmation related to paid reports
  • JEE Rank / Percentile — to generate your college prediction
  • Category, Gender, Home State, Branch Preference — to filter results accurately

We also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, and referral source
  • Device information (mobile, desktop, screen size)

💡 Note: We store form data (name, email, rank, preferences) in your browser's local storage to allow you to revisit your results. This data never leaves your device unless you submit the form.

⚙️
How We Use Your Information
Section 2

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To generate and display your personalised college prediction report
  • To process payments and verify paid report access via Razorpay
  • To send you your report or order confirmation via email (if applicable)
  • To improve and optimise our prediction algorithms and website experience
  • To analyse usage patterns through Google Analytics (anonymised)
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To respond to support queries sent to our email

We do not use your information for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you.

Google AdSense & Cookies
Section 3

CollegePIPE uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet.

  • Google may use cookies to personalise ads shown to you
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing history
  • The DoubleClick cookie is used by Google to serve interest-based ads

🔗 Opt out: You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

We also use Google Analytics (via Google Tag Manager) to understand how visitors interact with our site. This data is anonymised and aggregated — we cannot identify you personally from Analytics data.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies — Required for the predictor tool to function (local storage for form data)
  • Analytics cookies — Google Analytics to measure traffic and behaviour
  • Advertising cookies — Google AdSense to serve relevant ads

Data Sharing & Disclosure
Section 5

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We may share your data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We share data with Razorpay solely to process your payment. They are contractually bound to keep your data confidential.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business transfer: In the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

⚠️ We will never sell your email address, mobile number, or rank data to any educational institution, coaching centre, or marketing agency.

🔒
Data Security
Section 6

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse:

  • Our website is served over HTTPS (SSL/TLS encrypted)
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Razorpay — we never see or store card details
  • Form data is stored only in your browser's local storage and on Google's secure infrastructure
  • Access to any user data is limited to authorised personnel only

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
