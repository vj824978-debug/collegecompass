Our Mission

Make JEE Counselling Less Stressful for Every Student

Every year, over 12 lakh students appear for JEE Main. Most of them struggle during counselling — not because they don't work hard, but because the information is scattered, confusing, and often behind a paywall.

CollegePIPE's mission is to give every JEE aspirant — regardless of their coaching or background — access to accurate, data-driven college predictions completely free of cost.

We believe the right college information should be accessible, honest, and free for everyone.

Our Story

How CollegePIPE Was Born

😤
The Problem

Most predictor tools showed outdated data, required expensive subscriptions, or gave generic lists with no personalisation. JEE students deserved a better, completely free alternative.

🛠️
The Build

We collected and cleaned official cutoff data from JoSAA, CSAB, JAC, COMEDK and 7 other counsellings. We then built a prediction engine that accounts for category, gender, home state, branch, and round.

🚀
The Launch

CollegePIPE launched in April 2025. We cover 2,800+ colleges across 9 counsellings and 23 states — and we're still adding more. Our goal: be the most complete JEE counselling tool in India.

Where Our Data Comes From

We are committed to data accuracy and transparency. All cutoff data on CollegePIPE is sourced exclusively from official government portals. We update our database after every official counselling round.

We do not use estimated ranks, community-sourced data, or AI-generated cutoffs. If a data point cannot be verified from an official source, we don't include it.
